Wash me, maybe? Web Application | 2016

Wash Me Maybe? solves the age-old problem of washing your car just to have it rain the very next day. This simple app runs across all browsers and mobile devices, including Android and iOS phones and tablets. By using the magic of GPS and online weather forecasts, the app will automatically give a simple Yes/No answer on whether you should wash your car this instant. Additionally, the app also shows you a list of nearby car-wash locations, as well as a 10-day precipitation forecast.
